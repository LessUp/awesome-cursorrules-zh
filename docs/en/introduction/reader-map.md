# Reader Map

Different readers enter through different doors, but they should all converge on the same chain: **mission → architecture → guides → rules evidence → research**.

## Suggested routes

| Reader | First stop | Second stop | Third stop | Goal |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Interviewer / reviewer | [Project Mission](./mission) | [System Overview](../architecture/system-overview) | [Research References](../research/references) | Judge whether the repository shows real architectural maturity |
| Architect | [System Overview](../architecture/system-overview) | [Academy Path](../academy/learning-path) | [Composition Patterns](../rules/composition-patterns) | Understand how assets, narrative, theme, and evidence reinforce each other |
| Maintainer | [Maintainer Curriculum](../academy/maintainer-curriculum) | [Team Onboarding](../guides/team-onboarding) | [Rules Index](../rules/) | Keep content updates, fact sync, and publishing aligned |

## How to use the map

1. Use mission and research when you are making a value judgment.
2. Use architecture pages when you are making a system judgment.
3. Use academy and guides when you are making an adoption judgment.
