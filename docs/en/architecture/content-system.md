# Content System

## The main problem

Documentation-heavy projects usually fail from excess, not scarcity.  
Without explicit structure, users do not know what to read first, why they are reading it, or what to do next.

## The new content domains

- **Whitepaper**: why the project matters
- **Architecture**: how the site and knowledge system are organized
- **Playbook**: how to adopt and operate the rules
- **Resources**: where to go next

## Bilingual strategy

Chinese remains the primary explanatory language, but both locales keep the same structural model so the site stays coherent.
