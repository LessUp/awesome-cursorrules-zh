# Operating Model

## Why an operating model matters

Once rules serve teams, they stop being personal preferences.  
They affect review quality, architecture boundaries, and delivery consistency, so they need lightweight governance.

## Minimum viable model

| Role | Responsibility |
| --- | --- |
| Rule maintainer | keeps templates and resolves conflicts |
| Project owner | decides what enters the repo |
| Contributor | reports issues and improves the corpus |

## Recommended loop

1. detect a gap or conflict,
2. update rule or docs,
3. validate in a real development cycle,
4. record the change for future contributors.
